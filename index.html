About Me

My name is Kris Abildgaard. I was born in Copenhagen, Denmark but have lived on and off in the US for the last 20 years. I have a BFA in commercial photography and graphic design and work as the Director of Marketing for two alcohol companies. Over the last couple years I have gotten the chance to design scopes for a couple of websites. Now I am curious about the inner workings of the coding that makes my designs come to life!
